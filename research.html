Research & Publications

Research Interests

My research primarily focuses on:

  • Large Language Models (LLMs) - Understanding and improving LLM capabilities
  • Natural Language Processing - Low-resource languages, code-mixed text, multilingual NLP
  • Knowledge Graphs - Entity alignment, knowledge graph enhancement
  • Explainable AI - Making AI decisions interpretable and transparent
  • Neuro-symbolic AI - Combining neural networks with symbolic reasoning
  • Resource and Evaluation - Benchmark creation and evaluation metrics for NLP
  • AMR and UMR Parser - Abstract Meaning Representation and Universal Meaning Representation parsing
